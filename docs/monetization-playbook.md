# Monetization Playbook - dv-regression-lab

This is a commercial planning artifact, not a revenue guarantee. It exists to turn an archived technical proof into a clear, ethical selling motion.

## Commercial Lane

- **Lane:** Semiconductor DV regression control tower
- **Primary buyer:** ASIC verification managers, EDA platform teams, CI owners, and silicon program leads.
- **Why this can sell:** DV teams need a clearer loop from nightly regression evidence to flaky-test triage and operator handoff.

## Offer Ladder

1. **Regression triage assessment** - $8k-$20k. Review current regression logs, flake posture, and evidence retention gaps.
2. **DV control-tower pilot** - $40k-$150k. Adapt the scheduler, classifier, and evidence pack to one verification block.
3. **EDA workflow extension** - $100k+/year. License or maintain the triage workflow as part of a platform toolchain.

## Acquisition Channels

- Semiconductor engineering articles
- EDA and CI platform communities
- Partner referrals from reliability tooling

## Proof Gates Before Selling

- Regression examples remain synthetic or permission-safe
- Flake and failure taxonomy is explicit
- Stage-pilot relationship is described as the active runtime lane

## Risk Boundaries

- Do not claim silicon design ownership
- Keep customer logs out of public artifacts
- Avoid production readiness claims without partner validation

## 30/60/90 Day GTM

- **30 days:** Write a DV flake-cost calculator
- **60 days:** Create a sample nightly-regression walkthrough
- **90 days:** Offer a paid assessment to one verification team

## Metrics That Matter

- Assessment calls
- Nightly triage time saved
- Flake detection precision
- Pilot renewal

## Positioning Note

Keep the repository's archived status visible. Sell the reusable workflow, diagnostic, enablement material, or pilot path that the proof makes credible; do not sell the archive as an unsupported production service.
